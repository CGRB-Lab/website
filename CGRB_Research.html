Research

The primary focus of our laboratory is directed towards:

1. Employing an integrated approach to understand the role of RNA binding proteins in regulating a wide-variety of cellular processes and diseases such as bacterial infections, cancer and neurological disorders.

2. Developing computational tools for analysing genome-wide deep sequencing data to investigate genomic aberrations, which will allow early diagnosis of diseases such as cancer and haematological malignancies.

Cancer Genomics and RNA Biology Lab, Indian Institute of Technology, Hyderabad
